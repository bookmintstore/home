Privacy Policy

At EBookArc, we respect your privacy and value the trust you place in us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and purchase ebooks.

When you browse our website or buy ebooks, we may collect your name, email address, and payment details. We only use this information to process orders, improve user experience, and keep you updated about new releases or offers.

Cookies

We use cookies to improve navigation, analyze traffic, and provide personalized recommendations. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features of the site may not work properly without them.

Refunds & Special Offers

We believe in delivering value. Refunds are not offered on all purchases, but a few lucky users who have bought more than 5 ebooks from our website may be eligible for a surprise refund. These refunds are processed randomly and are part of our loyalty rewards.

During special occasions and festivals, we may also run exclusive sales and discounts. These limited-time offers are subject to change without prior notice.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to keep your information safe. Transactions are processed securely, and we never share your personal details with unauthorized parties.

Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. We will do our best to respond quickly and fairly.
